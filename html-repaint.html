html repaint

HTML Repaint is in a sense like repainting over an existing document. Changes are made to the "skin" without affecting the existing lay out. Repainting could include background color, out line, and visibility.

Most of my research described html repaint as expensive, this is in terms of performance (speed & smoothness) and the direct object model must also be considered for this very reason.
