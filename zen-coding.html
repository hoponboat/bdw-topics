Emmet & Zen Coding

Two methods of quick coding.

Zen Coding

Zen coding in a plugin used to code in effienct manner. It is essentially a short hand form of coding consiting of two components, an abbreviation expander and context-independent HTML-pair tag matcher. I think these are also called snippits and abriviations. Example below:

div#page>div.logo+ul#navigation>li*5>a

Emmet

Zen Coding is now referred to as emmet. Smashing magazine described the plugin as the most productive and time-saving text-editor plugin you will ever see.
